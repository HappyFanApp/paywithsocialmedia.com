SOCIAL MEDIA ASSET VALUE
These actions are based on exchanging value, whether it's a message, like, follower, or contact information, in return for receiving a benefit or exclusive access.

"Pay with a Tweet" is a marketing strategy where users are offered the opportunity to receive a product, download, or access to exclusive content in exchange for sharing a message on Twitter. Users share the message and, in return, they receive the promised benefit, thus generating greater dissemination and visibility for the brand or product in question.

"Pay with a Like" Users give a "like" to a post or page on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, or YouTube in exchange for accessing specific content or receiving a benefit.

"Pay with a Post" Instead of sharing a message on Twitter, users share a post on Facebook, Instagram, or any other popular social media platform.

"Pay with a Follow" Users follow an account on social media platforms such as Instagram, Twitter, or TikTok to gain access to exclusive content or special promotions.

"Pay with an Email" Users provide their email address in exchange for obtaining a specific resource, content, or download.
